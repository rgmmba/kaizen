About RGM

Rajeev Gandhi Memorial College of Engineering and Technology in Nandyal, Andhra Pradesh, is a top engineering college inaugurated in 1996. It's accredited by NBA and NAAC, with Autonomous status since 2010. Recognized for its quality education, it offers various skill development programs, is ISO certified, and has received numerous accolades and recognitions, including from organizations like TCS and DSIR. It's committed to industry relevance, employability, and continuous improvement.

About MBA

The Department of Management Studies at Rajeev Gandhi Memorial College of Engineering and Technology was established in 2006, offering MBA programs in Marketing, Finance, and HRM. It boasts qualified faculty, research initiatives, and modern teaching methods, including Wi-Fi connectivity and LCD projectors. The department emphasizes practical exposure through industry projects, seminars, workshops, and cultural events. It has affiliations with organizations like HMA and ISTD, providing opportunities for students' professional growth. With a focus on quality education and research, it aims for excellence in grooming future global managers..
